Latitude vs. Wind Speed

One thing that I noticed immediately was the lack of correlation between a region's distance from the equator and the wind speeds that region experiences. However, the sample size was weighted in favor of regions north of the equator. Disregaring this, one could derive outlier winds are more likely in the northern hemisphere vs. the southern.
